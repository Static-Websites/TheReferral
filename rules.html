How the Program Works

To earn the right to to chose referrals, we allow users to be initiated with a hot item, all you need to do is sign up for the TheReferral Rewards Program by providing your name, a valid email address, and accepting the program Terms & Conditions. You can then start referring your friends and followers to the product with your unique discount code. You will have access to the square site to see the sells orders used with your code. After you earn a gold status when selling more than 10,000 units you can invite one person to participate in TheReferral.

Rules for Referrers

You must be a current USA resident.

You need to be 18 years old or order and accept the program Terms & Conditions.

Must agree to collect your rewards only after the product has been deliver and if returns are made, you will ask to refund the reward.

You cannot refer yourself.

Referral Period Rules

A referee must accept their referral offer within 30 days of receiving your code or until the product is available.

If a referee forgets to use your code, you will not receive the reward.
